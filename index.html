Bong Cook - Multi Cuisine Restaurant

Bengal’s love for food is well known across the globe. For Bong Cook, this love or Rasana has worked like magic in inspiring our curated menu of authentic Bengali cuisines. Be it our signature Dab Chingri (a melt-in-the-mouth preparation of prawns roasted inside tender coconut) or the traditional Kasha Mangsho, Traditional vegetarian dishes like Doi Patal, Bati Chocchori and many more.
